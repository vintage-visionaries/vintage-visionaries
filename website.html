About Vintage Visionaries

Visionaries Event & Co. is a renowned event management company, situated in a cultural heritage city named Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, since 2018. The company emphasizes innovation with a blend of tradition, mentioning its motto as "Curating the Past, Designing the Future."

Vintage Visionaries has a large portfolio of expertly overseeing events varying from small get-togethers to large-scale events like weddings, conferences, cultural events, etc, establishing itself as a well-reputed firm within the city and the country. The company's commitment to authenticity and legacy sets it apart from other event management companies. Apart from hosting a client event, Vintage Visionaries also organizes its own cultural events to showcase India's local values and its culinary expertise.

Our Story

Founded by Nishtha Khandelwal

In the historic lanes of Lucknow, where tradition meets modernity, a vision for creating extraordinary celebrations was born. Growing up surrounded by the rich cultural heritage of the City of Nawabs, our founder was deeply influenced by the grandeur of traditional celebrations and the elegance of bygone eras.

After completing her education in event management and working with established firms, she recognized a gap in the market â€“ there was no company that truly captured the essence of vintage charm while incorporating modern event management techniques. In 2018, she took a leap of faith and founded Vintage Visionaries Event & Co.

Starting with just a small team and a big dream, the first event was a heritage-themed wedding that perfectly blended Lucknowi traditions with contemporary elegance. Today, Vintage Visionaries stands as a testament to the vision of preserving cultural heritage while creating modern, unforgettable experiences, driven by the same passion that inspired its founding.
